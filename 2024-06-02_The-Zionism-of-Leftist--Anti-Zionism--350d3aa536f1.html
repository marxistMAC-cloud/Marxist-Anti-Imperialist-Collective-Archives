The Zionism of Leftist “Anti-Zionism”

When perusing various leftist “anti-zionist” forums one will inevitably come across such claims as “As a Jew i’m anti-zionist”, “Remember, judaism does not equal Zionism!” or even “Zionism isn’t Judaism”(!).

It seems that in the extreme fear of being called anti-semites, these leftists have unconsciously (or consciously in many instances) adopted the zionist agenda, and become the radical apologists of it. This overcompensation to counter claims of anti-semitism by constantly making the point of “judaism is seperate from zionism”, prefacing all criticisms of zionism and Israel with “not all jews” and kissing the ring of the global jewry at every conceivable point precisely furthers the agenda of global zionism.

What is zionism other than honest and open judaism? Violent chauvinism towards all nations in favor of the made up “jewish nation”, and Israel is the materialization of this ideology. The leftist zionist simply leaves out the violent part, yet retains the essence of zionism, that jews constitute a nation, which itself denies the existence of nations and is supreme cosmopolitanism. This in itself forms an inner contradiction in zionism, between the emerging Hebrew nationalists and the zionists, but this is another topic. This helps the zionist agenda :

1) by confiscating the Jewish Question, one cannot at the same time be pro-jew/judaism and at the same time uphold an ideology that denies the existence of the jews as more than an ideology, ie. anti-zionism.

2)In the long run the leftist with his anti-nationalist rhetoric supports the global jewry in its goal of destroying nations (something certain flavors of communism, like trotskyists, openly support).

3) by associating anti-zionism in the West with the effeminate and unlikeable hippies and intellectuals, effectively alienating the masses from anti-zionism, this is further enforced by the associating of anti-zionism with islamism, largely done by muslim immigrants in the West. This leads to a situation where a principled anti-zionist in the West will likely not want to voice his disdain for Israel out loud, so as not to be grouped together with the irritating liberal crowds, although he’d likely be called a nazi by said liberals if he did.

The leftist “anti-zionist” thus isn’t an anti-zionist at all, but merely the left wing of zionism, seeking to rehabilitate zionism from the violent image given to it by the state of Israel. They implicitly support zionism with its thesis on nations, and violently oppose actual anti-zionism which posits that there can be no anti-zionist jew, as the entire jewish identity, be it the religious type or the secular one based on pseudo science about “jewish DNA”, is based on zionism and cannot exist outside of it. These leftist “anti-zionists” are happy then to join the zionists in calling anti-zionists nazis, and demanding their censoring by the imperialist bourgeoise.

Real anti-zionism demands the abolition of the Israeli state and the assimilation of the global jewry into their respective nations, with force or without. To free the world from zionism, we must free the world of judaism. The words written by Marx almost 200 years ago are still true today;
