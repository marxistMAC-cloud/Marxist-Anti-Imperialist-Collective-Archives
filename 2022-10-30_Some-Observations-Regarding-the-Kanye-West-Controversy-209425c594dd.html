The reader has perhaps heard of the recent controversy surrounding Kanye West, a popular musician from the United States. West has a reputation for his high-profile manic episodes: he will be tame for a year or so, and then go through a period wherein he insists the Clintons are going to kill him, the Kardashians are trying to steal his children, etc. Then, he’ll be committed to a mental hospital, disappear for a while to be put on mind-altering medications, and then come out ‘normal’ again. All of this goes back to that bizarre MKULTRA/MONARCH structure which sits behind modern bourgeois media, but that is a topic for another time (the reader is recommended to give the blog Vigilant Citizen a look if they’re curious).

West’s most recent episodes have been increasingly political. He made an appearance with Trump a few years back, and even declared his own candidacy for president in 2020. Just a couple weeks ago, he caused an uproar by wearing a shirt that read “White Lives Matter” in public. In each of these cases, West seemed impervious to the public backlash which ensued. That is, until he decided to offer his opinions regarding God’s Chosen.

On 7 October, West appeared on Fox News to talk about how Jared Kusher acted as former President Donald Trump’s “handler” in political affairs for the sake of “making money.” (1) Kusher was Trump’s son- in-law, being married to Ivanka Trump. Kusher is Jewish; Ivanka converted to Judaism after marrying him, and their wedding was officiated by Yitzchak Yosef, the Chief Rabbi of Israel. Rabbi Yosef insists that “math and science are nonsense” compared to Talmud studies (2); that, per the Talmud, Africans are a species of ape (3); and that goyim (non-Jews) should either serve the people of Israel or be deported to Saudi Arabia (4). His father, former Chief Rabbi Ovadia Yosef, said that “goyim have no place in the world; they were born only to serve the People of Israel.” (5) The older Yosef was close to President Reagan.

On 15 October, West appeared on the Drink Champs podcast and stated that Kushner “is an example of how the Jewish people have their hand in every single business that controls the world.” West added that “Jewish people have owned the Black voice,” and that “the Jewish community will take us and milk us until we die.” He made a series of other comments regarding “Jewish business secrets,” Jewish and Zionist control of media (“especially the music industry”), banks, politics, and so on. (6) Later, he added that it was a Jewish doctor who had him committed to a mental hospital, diagnosed with bipolar disorder, and put on medications.
