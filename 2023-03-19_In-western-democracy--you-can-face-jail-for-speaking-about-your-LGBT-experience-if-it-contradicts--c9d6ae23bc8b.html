In western democracy, it is not illegal to speak about genociding white people, about grooming kids into being LGBT e.t.c, but is illegal to even speak against the pushed narrative on LGBT, from people who had been LGBT themselves!

We are reaching new lows. There is a police report accusing ex-Homosexual Matthew Grech of illegally advertising “conversion therapy”. How did he do that? By recounting how he left the homosexual lifestyle behind him. It seems this was enough to trigger a top “equality” official in the European Union, Silvan Agius, who alongside Christian Attard, a founding member of the Malta LGTBIQ Rights Movement, and Cynthia Chircop, who co-chairs the organization, filled a report against Grech. Not only Grech is in court, no! It is also the journalists that interviewed him about his experiences. It seems, it is not the criminal only the one who shares his experience, but also the ones who record it.

Grech and the journalists face up to €5,000 in fines or up to five months in prison if convicted of violating Article 3 of Malta’s Affirmation of Sexual Orientation, Gender and Gender Expression Act, which makes it “unlawful for any person” to “advertise conversion practices.”
