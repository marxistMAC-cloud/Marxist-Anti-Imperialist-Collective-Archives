ANNOUNCEMENT : ISRAELI INVASION OF LEBANON

After the death of the Hezbollah leadership and the spectacular and continuous bombing of Lebanon, Israel decided to invade this state, this weakest link in the Arab nation, after Iran officially renounced the Axis of Resistance, offering everything to America in the hope of an easing of sanctions… A total betrayal, which will always be remembered as the total humiliation of Iran, which joins Saudi Arabia and the reactionary Gulf monarchies

We don’t know yet the real intentions of Zionist Entity for this new invasion or the scale of it (even if we see the psychological war done by Jerusalem Post about the “Greater Israel” encompassing Syria, Iraq… and Lebanon, or the multiple sales of Lebanese houses to Israeli new settlers) but we know enough about them to understand the danger for the entirety of Lebanese People, not only for Hezbollah, even if Israel pretends that they just want to destroy some infrastructures. Any inch of land must not be let to the Jewish colonizers.

Our lines are clear: for now, the sole goal of a Lebanese person must be the defense of his homeland, in all its forms, whether directly (fighting within the Resistance militias throughout the South), or more indirectly (welcoming displaced populations, helping the mentally or physically wounded throughout the territory, making propaganda, or even moralizing people and their loved ones around them). The priority for any sane Lebanese person must be (formal or not) national independence and the fight against the invaders.

However, we know enough about the strategies of Israel to understand that sectarianism is their greatest weapon, between religions (Maronites against Sunnis, Druzes Shias, etc.), identities, fake or not (what is Lebanon? Syrian? Arab? Phoenician ? A thing I know enough about these identities is that they are not Israelis!). Any attempt at communautarism and division during this particular time shall be seen as a treacherous attempt to work for the enemy.

Lebaneses : Your Homeland is in Danger! You can’t give up ! Shall Victory await you!
