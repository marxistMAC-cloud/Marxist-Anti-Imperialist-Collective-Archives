A Little Review of the statement by the PCF on the Ukrainian Situation

With the war in Ukraine, we have seen a full attack in terms of propagandas, absurd lies and idiotic takes from pro-Imperialist agents. And the fact that Russian forces seem to fail to retake the lands from the Russian people in Ukraine is alarming for the actual anti-imperialist forces. But what is interesting with the Ukrainian situation is plain simple : this is the perfect occasion to identify the pro-Imperialist and the anti-Imperialist agents inside the “Left” and the “communist” forces, especially in imperialist Nations like Great Britain, America and France.

Some “communists” will probably explain to us that this is normal to no be right during the international, or political crisis because they’re surprising and so that communists can be forgiven for not being right during these troubled times. I will affirm the reverse : that this is in time of crisis, where the world stares at all the class and national movements that communists must be right.

This is why the support for the WW1, an inter-imperialist war and a time of crisis, was the main division point between “Socialists” and Communists in Western world including France, because this kind of “troubled decision in troubled time” made millions of dead, famines and economical crisis destroying completely the proletariats from each country. I will review the statement by the PCF, titled “Oser la paix”, released on the 11th of March 2023.

For the foreign communists who have an idealized mind on the situation, you probably still respect The French Communist Party as the Vanguard Party (created in 1920 following the Tours Congress against Social-Fascists from SFIO who voted for war credit in the name of a, obviously bourgeois and imperialist, “Sacred Union” to create a Party inspired by Jaurès and Lenin’s teachings) which fought for national independence against colonialism by being the first party advocating for the independence of colonies, and being against sexism with first elected women in legislative elections while they can’t even participate and vote, defended the workers struggles massive strikes which give the French workers 2 weeks of paid vacations, and 40 hours of working hours per week in 1934, was the main protagonist of the Résistance with the Front National de lutte pour la libération et l’indépendance de la France, and got rewarded for that by having been given the rights to write the Program of the Conseil National de la Résistance and rights to have ministers like Ambroise Croizat, with nationalizations, Sécurité Sociale, women’s right to vote, loi sur les comités d’entreprises and statute of civil service, before being rejected in 1947 to become the Party against the European Integration, the American domination, and the colonialism as the great Party of National Independence and of the Working Class…

But this Party is dead, since 1976, the great PCF abandoned the Dictatorship of the Proletariat, since 1979, it abandoned proletarian anti-imperialist internationalism and Leninism, and since 1994 it even abandoned Marxism itself (no more class struggles, fights against EU, demands for collectivization of means of production, basically everything) to become a puppet of the libertarian social-democrat PS. So, let’s see what is their opinion on the situation :

The war launched by Vladimir Putin against Ukraine on February 24 is odious and irresponsible. This choice is a crime against the sovereignty and territorial integrity of a country and against international law.

So we can see that the PCF supports blindly the camp of those who think that the war started because of Putin alone and not because of NATO or of Kyiv which literally bombed Russians for years.

It plunges the Ukrainian people into an unbearable nightmare and threatens the security of Europe and the world. The risk of an uncontrollable escalation increases every day. The situation is extremely serious. In such an interdependent world, this war is a failure for all, a failure for the collective security of Europe.

We can remark that they talk about a collective security for Europe against Russian invasion… maybe do they want an United European Army or a rearmed Germany (this is not like this is exactly what the PCF fought against with the Treaty establishing the European Defence Community in 1954, even becoming the main political force in France, even in Europe against it, with The Gaullists) ?

The political, economic and financial pressures and sanctions against the oligarchy must contradict the Putin regime to stop the war. We distinguish the sanctions (strong and targeted measures against the oligarchy) from the logic of blockade (stopping of imports) which would be a collective punishment against the Russian people.

I’ve never seen any embargo, blocus and/or economical sanctions in the history of Humanity where the people is not harmed by it.This is exactly the thing that the Americans explain to us for Iraq, Cuba or DPRK.This is an absurd fairytale.

Respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity is a foundation of international law. . Peace and fraternity between peoples,

When the artificial frontier separates some people who clearly speak the same language, have the same culture/history and should be one and the same Nation, we should understand that the National Question should be absolutely resolved. We can also see that the French “Communists” don’t actually analyze the national question as Marxist-Leninists but as liberals like “frontiers = nations” (so, according to their logics, the Arab Nation is not one Nation, because right now, these artificial countries such as Lebanon or Iraq have different frontiers). the union of workers around the world for their emancipation, the freedoms, sovereignty and independence of peoples are constitutive of the communist commitment and aim.

The war started by V. Putin violates international law. No one can question the independence and sovereignty of Ukraine.

Nobody questions it contrary to you who question the independence and sovereignty of the Russian people in Donetsk and Luhansk.
